Crown Hill Stone Supply, LLC

Providing quality landscape materials, masonry, and ready mixed concrete products to homeowners, contractors, and designers.

Locally owned in the heart
of the Wine Country

Crown Hill Stone Supply can service just about every city in Northern California using our own fleet of trucks and delivery personal. We provide quality landscape materials, masonry, and ready mixed concrete products to homeowners, contractors, and designers.

Our facility is stocked with the most exclusive natural and manufactured stone lines, as well as a large stock of concrete retaining walls and interlocking pavers. If you are looking for landscape supplies such as bark mulch, decorative rocks, or soils, Crown Hill Stone Supply is the place to go.

Come see our display gardens for ideas and previews of flagstone, natural stone thin veneer, cultured stone, synthetic turf, interlocking pavers, and retaining walls. Crown Hill can supply projects of any size, from residential backyard spaces to large commercial projects.

Why choose Crown Hill?

Our mission is to provide top quality natural stone, architectural concrete products, and landscape supplies at the best possible prices. We proudly support our community by hosting events and funding local businesses, schools, and initiatives. With three convenient locations, we make it easy for customers to access our products. Committed to sustainability, our fleet is California CARB compliant, reducing emissions and minimizing our environmental impact.
